Welcome to the Esho Stays Help Hub.

We’re here to make your stay as smooth and stress-free as possible. If you have a question, chances are it’s answered below. If not, our friendly team is just a message away, so don’t hesitate to get in touch!

Booking & Payment

What payment methods do you accept ?

We accept all major debit and credit cards. For corporate bookings, bank transfer options are also available upon request.

Is there a minimum stay requirement ?

Yes. A (X)-night minimum applies to most properties, though some locations may vary. Long-stay discounts kick in for weekly and monthly bookings.

Can I amend or cancel my reservation ?

Absolutely! Flexible booking options are available depending on the rate you selected. Just reach out to us directly, and we’ll help you adjust your stay.

During Your Stay

What amenities are included ?

All our properties come fully equipped–think fast WiFi, Smart TVs, kitchen essentials, quality linens, and thoughtful extras to make you feel at home.

What if something goes wrong during my stay ?

We’re just a call or WhatsApp away, 24/7. Whether it’s a leaky tap or a lost key, we’ll sort it out fast!

Is housekeeping included ?

Yes! For longer stays, weekly cleaning is included. Fresh towels and linens are available as needed–just ask.

Policies

Are pets allowed ?

Some of our properties are pet-friendly, so be sure to filter your search or let us know if you’re bringing your furry friend along.

Is smoking permitted ?

All of our accommodations are strictly non-smoking indoors. Designated outdoor areas may be available.

Is there a damage deposit ?

Yes. A refundable security deposit may apply. It’s held securely and returned after your stay, assuming no damage or extra cleaning is required.

What’s your cancellation policy ?

It varies by property and rate type. Flexible and non-refundable options are available–you’ll see full details before confirming your booking.

Still have questions ?

We’re happy to help!
